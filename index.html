FEATURED SHOWS

April 2020

A Raisin in the Sun

“A Raisin in the Sun” is a play by Lorraine Hansberry that depicts the life of the Youngers, a Black family in 1950s Chicago. The plot revolves around the family's differing dreams tied to a $10,000 insurance check from the deceased Mr. Younger's life insurance policy.

$45

A Raisin Before Time

In a whimsical twist to “A Raisin in the Sun”, the Youngers find themselves in a prehistoric version of Chicago, where their dreams are no longer about a $10,000 insurance check, but about surviving the dinosaur-infested neighborhood. Their daily struggles now include dodging T-Rex attacks, outsmarting cunning velociraptors, and figuring out if a stegosaurus can be convinced to help with household chores.

$45

A Raisin On The Moon

In the thrilling sequel to the dinosaur-infused “A Raisin Before Time”, the Youngers have barely caught their breath from outsmarting T-Rexes when they find themselves facing a new challenge - an alien invasion! The extraterrestrial forces, intrigued by Earth's prehistoric life, descend upon the city, and the Youngers, seasoned from their dinosaur encounters, rise to the occasion, turning their dino-battling tactics to outwit the alien invaders and save their dinosaur-ridden neighborhood.

$45

A Raisin Never Dies

In the next thrilling chapter of the Youngers' space saga, they find themselves back on the moon, this time joined by none other than martial arts legend Chuck Norris. The alien invasion has been thwarted, but a new threat emerges - robot cars from outer space! With their dinosaur-taming skills and Norris's martial arts prowess, the Youngers gear up for an epic showdown. The moon's surface becomes their battleground as they outmaneuver and outwit the robotic menace, proving once again that no challenge is too great for this extraordinary family.

$45
